# Product Guide

## Target Audience
- **Competitive Age of Empires II Players:** Seeking statistical matchup advantages and drafting tools.
- **Casual Players:** Looking to learn civilization strengths, weaknesses, and counter-mechanics.
- **Tournament Organizers:** Needing a reliable and professional drafting platform for events.

## Core Value Proposition
- **Strategic Insight:** Providing real-time, data-driven civilization "counter-pick" suggestions.
- **Professional Drafting:** Facilitating structured drafts (Captains Mode, Hidden Pick, Custom Presets) for ranked and tournament play.
- **Educational Resource:** Offering a comprehensive database of unit mechanics, tech trees, and win rates.

## Key Features (Existing & To Be Polished)
- **Interactive Drafting Tool:** Supports various modes (Ban/Pick, Blind, Custom) with real-time feedback.
- **Civilization Database:** Detailed stats, win rates, and dynamic counter-charts.
- **User System:** Profiles for tracking match history and drafting preferences.

## Immediate Objectives
- **Comprehensive QA & Stabilization:** Rigorously test all existing functionality to ensure zero bugs.
- **Feature Completion:** Identify and finalize features that are currently in a "basic" or "incomplete" state.
- **Refinement:** Polish the UI/UX to ensure a professional and seamless experience for all user types.
